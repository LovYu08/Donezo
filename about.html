About Donezo

Your all-in-one productivity hub designed to keep you organized and focused.

🌟 Introduction

Donezo is a comprehensive, browser-based productivity suite combined with an integrated utilities hub. We offer an array of seamless tools—ranging from Pomodoro timers and habit trackers to secure password generators and grade calculators—all within a single, unified interface.

🎯 Our Purpose

Our core purpose is to help users stay organized, focused, and efficient. We believe that switching between multiple apps interrupts flow state. Therefore, Donezo ensures that all essential tools—whether you are a student computing a grade or a general user managing a tight schedule—are easily accessible.

🔭 Vision

Donezo strives for continuous improvement. We emphasize simplicity, speed, and usability in all of our designs. Future iterations will introduce more synchronized features, deeper local data persistence, and expanded cross-platform utilities, while never compromising our clean aesthetic.

💻 Developer Note

Donezo is built as a personal project with a primary focus on learning, development, and real-world application. It serves as an exploration of modern web development, UI/UX conceptualization, and local data persistence via the browser. Thank you for using and supporting this journey!
