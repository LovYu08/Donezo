Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Donezo. We are highly committed to respecting and protecting your privacy while you use our productivity and utility tools hub. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we safeguard your personal data when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

Donezo is designed to be as minimally invasive as possible. We do not directly collect, harvest, or request personal identifying information (PII) to operate our core services.

  • Optional Data: If you use our contact forms or email us directly, we may collect your name, email address, and the contents of your message solely for communication purposes.
  • Automatic Data (Non-Personal): When you visit the site, certain third-party integrations may automatically collect non-personal device, usage, and analytical data (such as IP addresses, browser types, and timestamp metrics).

3. Use of Local Storage

The core functionality of Donezo—including your custom Tasks, Habits, Daily Planner entries, Notes, and Timer presets—is exclusively saved to your browser’s local storage (localStorage).

This data remains localized on your physical device. It is not transmitted, synced, or stored on any external servers operated by Donezo. Clearing your browser cache or switching devices will reset this data.

4. Cookies and Web Beacons

We use "cookies" (small text files safely stored on your device) to enhance your user experience, preserve theme preferences (such as Light or Dark mode), and analyze platform traffic. Third-party vendors and analytics providers may also place cookies on your browser to collect performance data.

5. Google AdSense

Donezo utilizes Google AdSense to display advertisements. To provide these ads, Google and its partners utilize specific cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to Donezo and other websites on the internet.

  • Google uses cookies to display personalized, relevant ads based on user behavior.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by navigating to the Google Ads Settings at any time.

Alternatively, you can opt out of certain third-party vendor's usage of cookies for personalized ad tracking by visiting www.aboutads.info.

6. Third-Party Privacy Policies

Donezo's Privacy Policy does not apply to other integrated advertisers or external website links. We continually advise you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers (such as Google Analytics or Google AdSense) for more detailed information on their practices. We cannot control the activities of such other advertisers or websites.

7. Data Protection

We take all reasonable security measures to protect the integrity of the ecosystem. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. While we strive to protect any exchanged information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security against external breaches.

8. Children's Information

Adding protection for children while using the internet is a priority. Donezo does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided sensitive information to our website, please contact us immediately so we can remove such information from our end. We strongly encourage parental supervision for younger individuals utilizing productivity tracking tools over the web.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may occasionally update our Privacy Policy to reflect changing structural updates or legal requirements. These changes are effective immediately upon being posted on this webpage. Users are highly encouraged to review this page periodically for any modifications.

10. Consent

By actively using and browsing the Donezo website and tool offerings, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms and conditions.

11. Contact Information
