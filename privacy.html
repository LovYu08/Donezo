Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026. Review how we handle your data.

📝 Introduction

Donezo respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding any information collected when you use our website or web application.

🗄️ Data Collection

Currently, Donezo does not actively collect or harvest personally identifiable information (PII) directly to our servers. Your usage of the application is largely localized unless specified otherwise.

💾 Local Storage (Your Data)

Any data generated inside the Productivity Hub—including your Tasks, Habits, Pomodoro records, Planner Schedules, and Notes—is stored completely locally within your browser's `localStorage` object. This means your data belongs entirely to you and remains exclusively on your device. We have no backend access to view, process, or sell this locally-stored data. If you clear your browser cache, you will lose this generated data.

🌐 Third-Party Services

In the future, and to maintain the platform, Donezo may implement third-party analytics (like Google Analytics) or integrated advertising components (like Google AdSense). These services may use standard cookies or tracking scripts to serve personalized content. You will be prompted regarding cookie policies once these are officially integrated.

🔄 Updates to Policy

We reserve the right to modify or adjust this Privacy Policy at any time. When updates happen, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this respective document. Continued use of Donezo after any updates implies acceptance of the new policy.

✅ Consent

By accessing and utilizing the Donezo platform, you explicitly consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to our terms.
